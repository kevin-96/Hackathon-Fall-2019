Loan Information

A loan is borrowed money from a financial institution or business, which is charged interest over the period of time in which it is being repayed. The following videos will help you understand what the different types of loans are, as well as the different rates and how your repayment option matters in the lifetime of the loan.

Types of Loans

There are various types of loans: Student loans, mortgage, business loans and car loans. Mortgages and car loans are both loans given to buy a certain product; a house or a car respectively. Student loans are given to people who wish to attend a university and need the money for tuition. business loans are given to people who need money to start a business venture. The video will go more in depth on these loan types.

Fixed vs Variable Rates

Fixed rate loans are loans given with an interest rate that does not change over time. Variable rates have an interest rate that does change throughout time, either increasing or decreasing, depending on the economy. The video will discuss the pro's and con's of both rate types.

Cryptocurrency Information

Cryptocurrency is a strong, decentralized currency that is mined by users. The process of mining involves calculating hash values until you get a collision and are then given the crypto coin. Cryptocurrency is not trackable, and has a limited amount in the market which cannot be increased, unlike banknotes which can be printed.

What is Cyrpto-Mining?

Crypto-Mining is the process of calculating hash values until you get a collision. As more crypto coins are mined, it becomes harder and harder to find a collision. Therefore, mining is often preformed in a group, where each member dedicates a piece of hardware to performing the needed calculations. Crypto-Mining is also done on the GPU, as it can perform calculations faster than the CPU.
